Dosa Recipe

  • Home

Ingredients

  • Idli rice - 2 cups
  • Urad dal (black gram lentils) - 1/2 cup
  • Fenugreek seeds - 1 teaspoon
  • Salt - to taste
  • Oil - for cooking

steps

  1. Wash and soak idli rice, urad dal, and fenugreek seeds in water separately for 6-8 hours or overnight.
  2. Drain the water from each and grind them separately to a smooth paste using a little water as needed.
  3. Mix the ground rice and urad dal batter together, add salt, and mix well. The batter should be of pouring consistency.
  4. Cover the batter and let it ferment in a warm place for 8-12 hours or until it doubles in volume.
  5. Heat a non-stick or cast iron skillet over medium heat. Once hot, pour a ladleful of batter onto the skillet and spread it in a circular motion to form a thin dosa.
  6. Drizzle some oil around the edges and cook until the bottom turns golden brown.
  7. Flip the dosa and cook the other side until light golden brown and crisp.
  8. Repeat the process with the remaining batter.
  9. Serve hot dosas with chutney and sambar.

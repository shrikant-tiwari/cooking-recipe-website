Dum aloo recipe

  • Home

Ingredients

  • 500 grams baby potatoes
  • 2 cups whisked curd
  • 1 tablespoon Kashmiri red chilli powder
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • 1 tablespoon ghee
  • 1 tablespoon fennel powder
  • 1 tablespoon coriander powder
  • 1 teaspoon ginger powder
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon powder
  • Â½ teaspoon turmeric powder
  • Water (as required)
  • Salt to taste
  • Pinch of garam masala
  • Fresh coriander leaves (chopped)

steps

  1. Boil the baby potatoes until tender. Peel them off and prick them with a fork or toothpick.
  2. Heat oil and ghee in a pan. Fry the potatoes until golden brown and crisp.
  3. In the same pan, add fennel powder, coriander powder, cinnamon powder, ginger powder, red chilli powder, and turmeric powder. Add a little water and cook the spices well.
  4. Slowly add the curd, salt, and keep stirring continuously.
  5. Add the fried potatoes, garam masala, and cover the pan. Cook for 2-3 minutes.
  6. Garnish with fresh chopped coriander leaves and serve hot with chapati, paratha, naan, or rice.

idli sambhar recipe

  • Home

Ingredients

  • Toor dal (split pigeon peas) - 1/2 cup, washed and soaked for 30 minutes
  • Vegetables (carrot, potato, drumsticks, etc.) - 1 cup, chopped
  • Onion - 1, chopped
  • Tomato - 1, chopped
  • Green chilies - 2, slit
  • Tamarind - small lemon-sized ball, soaked in water
  • Sambar powder - 2 tablespoons
  • Mustard seeds - 1 teaspoon
  • Cumin seeds - 1 teaspoon
  • Curry leaves - a few
  • Asafoetida (hing) - 1/4 teaspoon
  • Red chili powder - 1/2 teaspoon
  • Turmeric powder - 1/2 teaspoon
  • Salt - to taste
  • Oil - 2 tablespoons
  • Fresh coriander leaves - for garnishing

steps

  1. Pressure cook toor dal with chopped vegetables, onion, tomato, and green chilies until soft.
  2. Extract tamarind juice from soaked tamarind and keep aside.
  3. In a separate pan, heat oil and add mustard seeds. Let them splutter.
  4. Add cumin seeds, curry leaves, and asafoetida. Sauté for a minute.
  5. Add sambar powder, red chili powder, and turmeric powder. Sauté for a minute.
  6. Add the cooked dal mixture, tamarind juice, and salt. Mix well and bring to a boil.
  7. Simmer for 10-15 minutes until the flavors meld together.
  8. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
  9. Serve hot with idli or dosa.

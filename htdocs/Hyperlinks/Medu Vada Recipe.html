medu vada  recipe

  • Home

Ingredients

  • Urad dal (black gram lentils) - 1 cup, soaked for 4-6 hours
  • Green chilies - 2, finely chopped
  • Ginger - 1 inch piece, grated
  • Cumin seeds - 1 teaspoon
  • Curry leaves - 8-10 leaves, finely chopped
  • Onion - 1 medium, finely chopped
  • Salt - to taste
  • Oil - for deep frying

steps

  1. Drain the soaked urad dal and grind it into a smooth paste using very little water.
  2. Transfer the ground dal into a bowl and add chopped green chilies, grated ginger, cumin seeds, curry leaves, chopped onion, and salt. Mix well.
  3. Heat oil for deep frying in a pan over medium heat.
  4. Wet your hands with water, take a small portion of the batter, and shape it into a donut-like vada.
  5. Gently slide the shaped vada into the hot oil and fry until golden brown and crispy on all sides.
  6. Remove the fried vadas using a slotted spoon and drain excess oil on a paper towel.
  7. Serve hot with chutney and sambar.

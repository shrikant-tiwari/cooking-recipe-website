shahi paneer recipe

  • Home

Ingredients

  • Paneer (cottage cheese) - 250g, cut into cubes
  • Onion - 2, finely chopped
  • Tomato - 2, pureed
  • Cashew nuts - 15-20, soaked in water
  • Yogurt - 1/2 cup
  • Green chilies - 2, finely chopped
  • Ginger-garlic paste - 1 tablespoon
  • Cardamom powder - 1/2 teaspoon
  • Cinnamon powder - 1/2 teaspoon
  • Red chili powder - 1 teaspoon
  • Turmeric powder - 1/2 teaspoon
  • Garam masala - 1 teaspoon
  • Cream - 2 tablespoons
  • Oil - 2 tablespoons
  • Salt - to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves - for garnishing

steps

  1. Heat oil in a pan and sauté chopped onions until golden brown.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste and green chilies. Sauté for a minute.
  3. Add tomato puree, cashew nuts, turmeric powder, red chili powder, cinnamon powder, cardamom powder, and salt. Cook until oil separates.
  4. Whisk yogurt and add it to the pan. Cook until the mixture thickens.
  5. Add paneer cubes and simmer for 5-7 minutes.
  6. Stir in cream and garam masala. Cook for another 2-3 minutes.
  7. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
  8. Serve hot with naan or rice.

white sauce pasta recipe

  • Home

Ingredients

  • Pasta (penne, fusilli, or any preferred shape) - 250g
  • Butter - 2 tablespoons
  • All-purpose flour - 2 tablespoons
  • Milk - 2 cups
  • Garlic - 2 cloves, minced
  • Onion - 1 small, finely chopped
  • Black pepper powder - 1/2 teaspoon
  • Nutmeg powder - a pinch
  • Salt - to taste
  • Grated cheese (optional) - for garnish
  • Fresh parsley or basil leaves - for garnish

steps

  1. Cook pasta in boiling salted water according to package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a pan, melt butter over medium heat. Add minced garlic and chopped onion. Saut√© until onions are soft and translucent.
  3. Add all-purpose flour and cook for 2-3 minutes until it turns slightly golden, stirring continuously.
  4. Gradually pour in milk while whisking continuously to avoid lumps. Cook until the sauce thickens and coats the back of a spoon.
  5. Season with salt, black pepper powder, and a pinch of nutmeg powder. Stir well.
  6. Add cooked pasta to the white sauce and toss until evenly coated.
  7. Remove from heat and serve hot.
  8. Garnish with grated cheese (if using) and fresh parsley or basil leaves.
